Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: December 2025

Global Chat is committed to maintaining a safe environment. We have a Zero Tolerance Policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and the exploitation of children.

1. Age Requirement

Our service is strictly for users aged 18 and older. We employ an age-gating mechanism upon entry. Any user found to be under 18 will be banned immediately.

2. Policy Against CSAM/CSAE

We strictly prohibit the transmission, distribution, or solicitation of any content that depicts child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes text, images, or links.

3. Reporting Mechanism

We provide in-app tools for users to report safety concerns:

  • Users can tap the "Flag/Report" icon inside any chat session to report abuse immediately.
  • Reports are processed, and offending IP addresses are banned.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

If we become aware of any content involving CSAM, we will:

  • Immediately ban the user.
  • Preserve the relevant data (IP address/logs) where technically possible.
  • Report the incident to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.
